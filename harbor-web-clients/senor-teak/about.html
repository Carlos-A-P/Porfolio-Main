Company History

What started as a father and sons team has grown into a trusted name in furniture restoration. Every piece tells a story, and we're here to help preserve those memories.

A Family Business Beginning

Señor Teak began as a small family operation with a simple mission: to treat every piece of furniture with the same care and dedication we'd give our own family's treasured pieces. Our father taught us that quality craftsmanship isn't just about skill—it's about heart, patience, and respect for the materials we work with.
