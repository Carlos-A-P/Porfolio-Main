Our Vision for the Future:

We envision a community where faith transforms lives and spreads hope throughout the world. Our vision is to create lasting impact through compassionate service, meaningful worship, and authentic relationships that reflect Christ's love to all people.

  • ✝ Expanding our reach to serve more communities in need.
  • ✝ Building stronger connections through fellowship and worship.
  • ✝ Nurturing the next generation of faithful leaders.
  • ✝ Creating spaces where all feel welcomed and valued.

Core Values We Live By:

Our values guide every decision we make and every action we take. Rooted in scripture and lived out daily, these principles shape our church community and define who we are as followers of Christ.

  • ✝ Love unconditionally as Christ loves us.
  • ✝ Serve others with humility and compassion.
  • ✝ Worship with authenticity and reverence.
  • ✝ Grow together in faith and discipleship.
